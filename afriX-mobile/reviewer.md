# My recommendation

Rather than just submitting the app, let's prepare **AfriX as if it's launching as a venture-backed fintech platform**.

That means creating a complete launch package:

1. **Google Play Store listing** (optimized for discovery and approval).
2. **Apple App Store listing** (tailored to Apple's review expectations).
3. **Privacy Policy** specific to AfriX's data handling.
4. **Terms of Service** aligned with the platform's user, merchant, and agent ecosystem.
5. **App Review Guide** explaining the app to reviewers and providing test credentials.
6. **Media Kit** including logos, screenshots, brand colors, and press materials.
7. **Launch Kit** with social posts, website updates, FAQs, and support documentation.

Taken together, these assets will make AfriX look like a mature digital commerce platform from day one—not simply a newly published app. I think that's the standard the product deserves.
