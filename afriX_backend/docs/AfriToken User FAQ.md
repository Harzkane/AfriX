## Getting Started

### What is AfriToken?

AfriToken is a peer-to-peer platform that lets you exchange digital tokens (NT and CT) with other users and agents. It works like this:

- Buy tokens from agents using Naira or XOF
- Send tokens to friends instantly
- Swap between different token types
- Sell tokens back to agents for cash

**Think of it like**: A digital marketplace for exchanging value across borders.

### Is AfriToken a bank?

No! AfriToken is a technology platform that connects you with:
- Other users (for peer-to-peer exchanges)
- Independent agents (for token-cash exchanges)

We don't hold your money, process payments, or provide banking services.

### How is this different from other apps?

**Traditional apps**:
- Go through banks (slow, expensive)
- High fees
- Weekends/holidays = delays
- Country restrictions

**AfriToken**:
- Direct token exchanges (fast)
- Lower fees (0.5-2%)
- Works 24/7
- Cross-border ready

### Is AfriToken legal?

Yes! We operate as a technology platform facilitating digital token exchanges. However:
- Tokens are NOT Nigerian Naira or XOF currency
- Tokens are blockchain-based digital assets
- You're trading tokens, not money
- Agents are independent contractors

**Always check your local laws about using digital tokens.**

### Do I need to verify my identity?

**Basic account** (email only):
- Can receive tokens
- Can send up to 10,000 NT/day
- Can swap tokens
- Limited merchant features

**Verified account** (with ID):
- Higher daily limits
- Full merchant features
- Priority support
- Better rates

### How do I create an account?

1. Download AfriToken app (Google Play / App Store)
2. Enter your email and create password
3. Verify your email (check inbox/spam)
4. Done! Start using the app

No bank account needed to start!

## Acquiring Tokens

### How do I get tokens?

**Three ways**:

1. **Buy from Agent** (most common):
   - Send Naira/XOF to agent
   - Agent mints tokens to your wallet
   - Takes 5-15 minutes

2. **Receive from Friend**:
   - Friend sends tokens to you
   - Instant (< 1 minute)
   - Small platform fee

3. **Swap Other Tokens**:
   - Have USDT? Swap to NT/CT
   - Instant exchange
   - 1.5% fee

### How to buy tokens from an agent?

**Step by step**:

1. **Select "Buy Tokens"** in app
2. **Choose token type** (NT or CT)
3. **Enter amount** (e.g., 10,000 NT)
4. **Pick an agent** from the list:
   - See ratings, response times
   - Choose based on your preference
5. **Send payment** to agent's account:
   - Bank transfer or mobile money
   - Agent's details shown in app
6. **Upload proof**:
   - Screenshot of transfer
   - Transaction reference number
7. **Wait for agent** to confirm (~5-15 min)
8. **Receive tokens** instantly!

### How do I choose a good agent?

### What if agent doesn't respond?

**Timeline**:

You can always try a different agent!

Yes! Two ways:

**Option 1**: Buy USDT from crypto exchange, then swap to NT/CT in AfriToken

## Selling Tokens

### How do I convert tokens back to cash?

**Sell to an agent**:

1. Tap "Sell Tokens"
2. Choose amount and token type
3. Select agent from list
4. Confirm sale
5. **Your tokens lock in escrow** (protected!)
6. Agent sends Naira/XOF to your account
7. Check your bank/mobile money
8. Confirm receipt in app
9. Done! Tokens burned, agent's capacity restored

**Protection**: Your tokens are in escrow. Agent can't access them until you confirm receipt!

### What is escrow protection?

**How it protects you**:
- Your tokens locked in smart contract (safe!)
- Agent CANNOT access them
- You have 30 minutes to confirm or dispute
- If agent doesn't send cash: Your tokens refunded automatically
- If you dispute: Tokens refunded, agent penalized

**You're protected throughout the process!**

### What if agent doesn't send me cash?

**Timeline**:
- 0-10 minutes: Normal processing time
- 10-20 minutes: Agent might be delayed
- 20-30 minutes: You can dispute if no payment
- After 30 minutes: Auto-dispute if you don't respond

**To dispute**:
1. Tap "I Didn't Receive It"
2. Explain what happened
3. Upload bank statement (optional)
4. Admin reviews within 24 hours
5. Decision made (almost always in user's favor if you're honest)

### Do I need to wait for agent confirmation?

When selling:
1. You initiate sale (tokens to escrow)
2. Agent sends you cash
3. **YOU confirm when you receive it**
4. Agent waits for YOUR confirmation
5. Transaction completes after you confirm

**You're in control!**

### Can I cancel after sending to escrow?

**Before agent sends cash**: Yes!
- Tap "Cancel" 
- Tokens refunded from escrow
- No penalty

**After agent sends cash**: No!
- Agent already sent you money
- Must confirm receipt or dispute
- Cancelling now = fraud

### What if I confirm but didn't receive cash?

**Don't do this!** Only confirm if you actually received cash.

If you confirm falsely:
- Transaction marked complete
- Cannot dispute after confirming
- You lose your tokens
- You might get flagged for fraud

**Always verify cash in your account before confirming!**

### Why are there fees?

Fees cover:

- Customer support

- Agent network costs

**Still cheaper than**:

**You can reduce fees by**:

**You cannot**:
- Remove fees entirely (platform needs to operate)
- Negotiate custom rates

### Are there hidden fees?

**No!** All fees shown before you confirm:
- Amount to send

- Total deducted
- Amount recipient gets

**Example screen**:

**Agents can**:

**Agents cannot**:
